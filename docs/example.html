2. Inline Markers

  • Bold: **bold** or __bold__
  • Italic: *italic* or _italic_
  • Strikethrough: ~~strikethrough~~
  • Superscript: X2: X<sup>2</sup>
  • Subscript: H2O: H<sub>2</sub>O
  • Underline:
    • underline with u tag: <u>underline with u tag</u>
